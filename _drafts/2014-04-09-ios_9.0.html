Gaia GPS iOS v9.0

With Gaia 9, we'd like to welcome all Gaia users to a new era of maps.

This update includes a blazing fast vector topo maps engine, that works offline and on, provides coverage worldwide, and brings a range of benefits, from vastly decreased download sizes, to amazing readability at any zoom level.

Besides the vector capabilities, the new engine will display all of the existing maps at much higher speed - in fact, we don't think they can get any faster. At 60 frames per second, the map display now rivals any high-performance video game.

Gaia GPS 9 brings us into the era of the living map - this is a map that changes every minute, impacted by your footsteps and the footsteps of those like you.

The map incorporates weekly updates of OpenStreetMap data, weekly updates from our Places crawler that finds reports from around the internet, and daily updated GaiaCloud tracks.

It's a new kind of map, and more, in Gaia 9:

  • vector topo maps
  • extremely fast display of all maps, raster and vector
  • all new routing and distance measuring interface
  • options to show track recorder and photo button on main map
  • display public GaiaCloud tracks from other users on the map
  • POI data and related reports have been updated and greatly expanded (clear cache in Settings)
